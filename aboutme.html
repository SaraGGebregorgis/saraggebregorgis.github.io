My Story

I come from a background where education, discipline, and hard work were deeply valued. From a young age, I always aimed to make my father proud, who worked at a school and was involved in administrative and data entry tasks.

I often helped him with entering data and organizing information, which made me very familiar with working with structured data early on. At the time, I didn’t realize it, but this was my first exposure to the kind of work I enjoy today.

I’ve always had a strong interest in mathematics and problem-solving, and I performed well academically. Coming from a challenging background, I didn’t always have access to extracurricular opportunities, so I focused on creating my own path through education, curiosity, and self-discipline.

Education

My journey into data didn’t follow a traditional path. I was first introduced to working with data at a young age while helping my father with data entry tasks at his school, an experience that later shaped my career direction. I later pursued Software Development, where I built a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and SQL, along with tools like Microsoft Excel, VS Code and GitHub

During my studies, I worked on projects that helped me understand how technology solves real-world problems. I developed skills in coding, structuring data, and logical thinking, and through working with different programming languages, I became comfortable quickly adapting to new technologies. As I explored further, I realized that what stood out most to me was working with data identifying patterns, solving problems, and turning information into meaningful insights.

Today, I am focused on data analytics, combining my technical skills and analytical thinking to build projects that reflect real-world impact and help me grow into a confident data analyst.

What Makes Me Different

What makes my journey unique is that my interest in data started early helping my father with data entry at school. What once felt like a small task became the foundation of what I truly enjoy today: working with structured data and turning it into meaningful insights.
